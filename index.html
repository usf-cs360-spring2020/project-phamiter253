Introduction

When trying to discover a new anime tv series, I usually get overwhelmed with choices, recommendations, and reviews. A good resource to use is MyAnimeList.com where completed, currently airing, and upcoming shows are listed and organized by genre, ranking, scores and etc.

MyAnimeList has extensive data about each anime in its catalog and all of the users who leave ratings and reviews. Each anime page has title, synopsis, air date, cast, production, ranking, ratings, and scores while a user profile includes their reviews, recommendations, and personal statics. Fortunately I have found a few datasets that crawled the websites information.

With these datasets, I want to identify trends between the years, ratings, and the users and maybe create an interactive recommendation graph.
