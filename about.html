Why Use Anime Dataset with Reviews?

As an anime watcher, I was very excited to discover this dataset. Now I can find random animes while working on classwork. When I saw there were many datasets mapping tons of shows' information, audiences' demographics, and reviews I thought that they should all be compiled and cross referenced. After I filter, wrangle, and organize these csv files, I want to plot a network of shows by the year and category and a map for the location of users with their gender and age statistics. Both graphs with interactivity for reviews and recommendations.
