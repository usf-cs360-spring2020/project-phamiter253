Above & Lowest Rated Animes

The amount of Above average and Below average rated animes that premiered in the years between 1986 and 2019 are visualized.


Interactivity

  • Stacked vs Grouped Bars

    • Change the bar graph layout with the raido buttons at the top.

  • Reference Line with Value

    • Mouseover any bar to display reference line and the corresponding value for that bar.

  • Brushing Ratings

    • Mouseover any bar to highlight bars of the same subgroup.

  • On Demand Details

    • Click on any bar and display the highest and lowest rated anime that premiered that year.

Discrete Totals

The height of the bars of represent the total amount of either Above Average or Below Average animes for each year.

Categorical Years

Each year represents the year an anime was released.

Observations

The amount of anime released each year increased almost each year.
In the late 90s and early 2000s, the majority of anime was rated average above average, especially in 2007.
I was expecting each year's highest rated anime to be recognizable, but early years like 1990, 1991, and 1994 have less popular animes as their highest rated anime.
The highest rated animes all have multiple genres listed and some of them are common and generic like Comedy, Action, Drama while the lowest rated anime have at most three genres and the most common genres are Music, Horror, and Dementia.
